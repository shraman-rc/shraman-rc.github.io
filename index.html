Deep Tensor Convolution on Multicores
with David Budden, Alex Matveev, Shibani Santurkar, Nir Shavit
International Conference on Machine Learning (ICML), 2017

We leverage Winograd-style Fast Fourier Transforms to minimize computation for high-dimensional convolutions. Our algorithm runs faster than other deep learning libraries (Caffe, TensorFlow, etc.) for both inference and training of 2D and 3D ConvNets on CPU.
